Homicides at Bogota D.C

In this visualization we show the number of homicides between 2010 and 2015 in the city of Bogota D.C, Colombia. The data was taken from the police department webpage.

There is a drop in the mean of homicides at the start of 2012, this could be due to the prohibition of bearing guns, which was adopted in that period by the local goverment.

Firearms and knives are the most prevalent type of weapons involved in homicides in the city of Bogota D.C.

Homicides with firearms are greater than homicides with knives for almost any hour in the day. However between 0:00 to 8:00 the number of homicides with firearms and knives are close to each others.
